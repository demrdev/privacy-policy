Privacy Policy - Geometry Calculator

Last Updated: June 3, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Geometry Calculator ("the App", "we", "our") handles user information. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, including children under 13 years of age.

Data Collection

Geometry Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The application operates entirely offline on your device and does not require an internet connection to function.

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Device identifiers or unique IDs
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Contact lists or calendar data
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Any form of user-generated content

How the App Works

Geometry Calculator is a standalone educational tool that:

  • Performs all calculations locally on your device
  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not store calculation history beyond the current session
  • Does not sync data across devices
  • Does not contain any social features or user interaction

Permissions

Our application requests only the minimal permissions required for basic functionality:

  • Storage (if applicable): Only to save calculation results locally on your device
  • No network permissions are requested or used

Third-Party Services

Google Play Services

While Geometry Calculator itself does not collect any data, it is distributed through Google Play Store, which may collect:

  • Basic app usage statistics (installation, updates, crashes)
  • Device information for compatibility purposes

For information about Google Play's data practices, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

This application does not contain any advertisements or marketing content.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics services or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

Geometry Calculator is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13:

  • The app does not collect any personal information from any user
  • No account creation or sign-in is required
  • There are no social features or communication capabilities
  • Content is educational and appropriate for all ages
  • We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

For Parents

Parents can feel confident that:

  • Your child's privacy is fully protected
  • No information about your child is collected or shared
  • The app can be used without parental supervision regarding data privacy
  • There are no in-app purchases or external links

Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to personal information through our app.

Your Rights

As we do not collect any personal data, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove it completely from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us:

Email: dmr.translate@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours.
