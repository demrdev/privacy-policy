This Privacy Policy explains the data practices of the wordbrain application ("the App", "we", "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children under 13 years of age.

Data Collection

wordbrain does not collect any personal data whatsoever. The application operates entirely offline on your device and does not transmit any information to servers or third parties.

  • Contact lists or calendar data
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Any form of user-generated content
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Advertising identifiers

How the App Works

wordbrain is a standalone word game that:

  • Functions entirely offline on your device
  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not store any gameplay data beyond the current session
  • Does not sync data across devices
  • Does not contain any social features or user interaction
  • Does not require internet connectivity

Permissions

Our application uses only the minimal permissions required for basic functionality and does not request any additional permissions.

Third-Party Services

wordbrain does not use any third-party services except for Google Play Services required for app distribution. Google Play Store may collect basic app usage statistics (installation, updates, crashes) for compatibility purposes. For information about Google Play's data practices, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

No Advertising

This application does not contain any advertisements, marketing content, or promotional materials.

No Analytics

We do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or performance monitoring systems.

Children's Privacy (Under 13)

wordbrain is specifically designed to be safe for children under 13 years of age:

  • The app does not collect any personal information from any user, regardless of age
  • No account creation or sign-in is required
  • There are no social features, chat functions, or communication capabilities
  • Content is educational and appropriate for all ages
  • We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • No advertisements or third-party tracking tools are used
  • No in-app purchases are available
  • No external links to websites or other apps

Information for Parents

Parents can be assured that:

  • Your child's privacy is fully protected - no data is collected
  • The app works completely offline and requires no internet connection
  • There are no in-app purchases or hidden costs
  • No external websites or links are accessible
  • Your child cannot communicate with other users
  • No personal information is ever requested
  • The app can be safely used without parental supervision regarding data privacy

Parental Controls: As we do not collect any data, there are no parental controls needed for data management. Parents can control app usage through device-level parental controls.

COPPA Compliance

This app complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since our app does not collect any data at all, it inherently protects children's privacy.

Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of data breaches, unauthorized access, or misuse of personal information through our app. All game progress is stored locally on your device and is deleted when the app is closed or uninstalled.

User Rights

As we do not collect any personal data:

  • There is no user data to access, modify, or delete
  • There is no data to port or transfer
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove it completely from your device
  • No opt-out mechanisms are needed as no data collection occurs

Legal Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with:

  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be:

  • Announced through app updates when applicable
  • Effective immediately upon posting
