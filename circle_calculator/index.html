Privacy Policy - Circle Calculator

Last Updated: October 10, 2025

Quick Summary: Circle Calculator does not collect, store, or process any personal data. Your privacy is completely protected.

1. Introduction

Circle Calculator is a completely offline mathematical calculation tool and does not collect any personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information. The application:

  • Does not require user registration
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Does not access your contacts, location, or photos
  • Does not track which calculations you perform
  • Does not use analytics services

3. Data Storage

The only data stored are your app preferences, saved locally on your device:

  • Theme preference (Dark/Light mode)
  • Decimal places setting (1-5 digits)
  • Language preference (English/Turkish)
  • Angle unit setting (Degrees/Radians)

No data is synced to cloud services or external servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data with third parties.

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics providers
  • No third-party services

5. Internet Connection

Circle Calculator works completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

6. Children's Privacy

Circle Calculator is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We comply with COPPA and GDPR-K regulations.

7. Security

Local data is stored securely using platform-standard encryption on your device.
