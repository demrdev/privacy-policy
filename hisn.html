Hisn - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Hisn ("we," "our," or "the App"). Hisn is an Islamic remembrance (adhkar) and prayer times application developed by Arif Edemir. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information.

Our Core Principle: Hisn is designed with a privacy-first approach. All your data stays on your device. We do not operate servers that collect, store, or process your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

Hisn collects minimal data, all of which remains on your device or within your personal iCloud account.

2.1 Location Data (When In Use)

We request access to your location only while the app is in use for the following purposes:

  • Prayer time calculation: To determine accurate prayer times based on your geographic coordinates.
  • Qibla direction: To calculate the direction of the Qibla from your current location.

Your location data is processed entirely on your device using the Adhan library. It is never transmitted to any server or third party.

2.2 Device Activity (Screen Time API)

Hisn uses Apple's Screen Time API (Family Controls / Managed Settings) to enable the app-blocking feature. This allows the App to:

  • Block selected apps until daily adhkar are completed.
  • Automatically unblock apps once the adhkar requirement is fulfilled.

Apple's Screen Time API is designed with privacy in mind. Hisn cannot see the names, bundle identifiers, or usage data of your apps. The API uses opaque tokens that only the system can resolve. No device activity data is transmitted off your device.

2.3 User Preferences

Your settings, adhkar progress, and preferences are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults (via an app group container). If you use multiple Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account, these preferences may sync via iCloud. This sync is handled entirely by Apple and does not pass through our servers.

2.4 Purchase Information

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. RevenueCat, our subscription management SDK, communicates with Apple's servers to verify purchase receipts and manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives only anonymized purchase data; it does not receive your name, email, or Apple ID.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • No account creation or login required
  • No analytics or usage tracking SDKs
  • No advertising SDKs or ad identifiers
  • No cookies or web tracking
  • No server-side data storage of any kind
  • No data shared with data brokers

4. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Accessed
RevenueCat Subscription management Anonymized purchase receipt data (via Apple)
Adhan Library Prayer time calculation None (runs entirely on-device)
Apple iCloud Preference sync across devices App preferences (managed by Apple)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

For RevenueCat's privacy practices, please visit: revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data persists as long as the App is installed on your device.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.
  • iCloud-synced preferences can be managed through your device's iCloud settings.

We do not retain any data on external servers, so there is no server-side data to delete.

6. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Apple's built-in device encryption and security features.
  • Apple's iCloud encryption for synced preferences.
  • The Screen Time API's opaque token system, which prevents apps from accessing identifiable information about other apps.

Since we do not operate servers or databases, there is no risk of a server-side data breach affecting your information.

7. Children's Privacy

Hisn does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The App does not require account creation and does not collect identifiable personal data from any user, regardless of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the email below. Since no personal data is collected by our systems, no action would typically be required.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including under GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations), you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you. (We hold none.)
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data. (Uninstalling the App achieves this.)
  • Opt-out: You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device's Settings.
  • Portability: Your data is stored in standard formats on your device.

9. International Users

Hisn is available worldwide. Since all data processing occurs on your device and no data is transferred to external servers, there are no cross-border data transfers initiated by us. Any iCloud syncing is governed by Apple's privacy policies and data processing agreements.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
