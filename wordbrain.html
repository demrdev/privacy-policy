Privacy Policy - WordBrain

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Quick Summary: WordBrain does not collect, store, or process any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device and does not transmit any information to servers or third parties.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how WordBrain ("the App", "we", "our") handles user information. WordBrain is a word puzzle game that operates entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information. The application:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Does not access your contacts, location, camera, or photos
  • Does not collect device identifiers or unique IDs
  • Does not use analytics services or tracking tools
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers

3. Data Storage

The only data stored are your app preferences and game progress, saved locally on your device:

  • Game progress and scores
  • User preferences and settings

No data is synced to cloud services or external servers. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data with third parties.

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics providers
  • No third-party services (other than Google Play Store for distribution)

5. Internet Connection

WordBrain works completely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

6. Permissions

The app requests only the minimal permissions required for basic functionality. No additional permissions beyond what is necessary for the app to run are requested.

7. Third-Party Services

WordBrain does not use any third-party services other than Google Play Store for distribution. Google Play Store may collect basic usage data such as:

  • Installation and update statistics
  • Device compatibility information
  • Crash reports

For information about Google Play's data practices, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

8. Children's Privacy

WordBrain is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13:

  • The app does not collect any personal information from any user
  • No account creation or sign-in is required
  • There are no social features or communication capabilities
  • There are no in-app purchases
  • There are no advertisements
  • Content is educational and appropriate for all ages
  • We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

9. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to personal information through our app. Local data is stored securely using platform-standard storage mechanisms.

10. Your Rights

As we do not collect any personal data, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove it and all associated data completely from your device.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page and/or announced within the app with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK)
